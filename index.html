CookiesBlock

Use CookiesBlock extension and browse modern webpages without losing sanity.

It disables popups like:

  • Notifications
  • Translations
  • Geolocalization ( enabled )
  • Cookies
  • Privacy policies & terms of use
  • Newsletters
  • 'Wait, there is more' etc.
